What is Information technology?

Technology has very many branches, Information Technology is one of them. IT is simply the use of computer systems to access information. Information Technology in its modern sense was first mentioned in an article published in 1958. The article argued that the new technology does not yet have a single established name but it shall be named Information technology. This defination can be categotised into 3;

Techniques for processing
Application of statistical & mathematical methods of decision making
Simulation of high-order thinking through computer programs

Some of the examples of Information technology include:

  • Personal computers
  • Video conferencing equipment
  • Telephone and radio equipment
